To My Love Princess Kyla E Cabaya,

In the gentle glow of the morning light, Your presence feels so warm, so bright. Each new day begins with you, My heart beats steady, strong, and true.

The stars above, in their quiet way, Try to capture your beauty every day. But their sparkle could never shine as deep, As the love you give, the joy I keep.

You’re the pen to my life’s blank page, Writing a story that grows with age. Every smile, a line of grace, Every touch, a safe embrace.

Even if the world turned upside down, If time stopped spinning all around, My heart would still hum your tune, And sing for you beneath the moon.

So here I am, with words to say, You are my light, my every day. In your love, I’ve found my start— The treasure etched into my heart.
